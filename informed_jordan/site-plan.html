Purpose

To inform the general population about the history and making of Jordan and Nike shoes. This information is to lead to greater understanding of the small things in life. This information is to be presented in a visually appealing.

Audience

For those who are generally interested in the history of Jordan shoes. Also for those who are interested in sneaker culture. Ages 8 to 70

Content

Home page

Welcome to your one-stop for all things Jordan sneakers! This website dives into the rich history behind iconic Jordan silhouettes, the Air Jordan 1, 4, and 11 in particular. Explore the stories behind these legendary shoes, from their initial design inspirations to their cultural impact. We'll take you through why they were created and how they became favorites on and off the court. So, lace up and get ready to learn all about the Jordan legacy! This website will continue to grow with more and more rich history, eventually expanding to Nike sneakers! Feel free to go to the Sneaker Adder in the navigation menu, to add more sneakers we good implement into the website!

There will be three other informative pages with specified sneaker information

Jordan Silhouettes

Fun Fact: In 1985, you could buy a pair of original Jordan 1 highs for just $64.99!
